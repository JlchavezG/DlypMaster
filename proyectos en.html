MAIN PROJECT : V.FAST

V.FAST

With VFAST we seek to create an online site where you can choose a place to visit in a safe way, presenting different functions in order to make the visit pleasant, thus creating new and better experiences.

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

Helping to maintain the sustainability of those places that have declined due to the pandemic caused by COVID-19, as well as bringing families together again, uniting groups of friends and motivating them to go out to little-known places that might seem interesting to them.
