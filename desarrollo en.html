DOCUMENTATION: V.FAST

Name / Logo

|| V.FAST = Visit Fast ||

-- Logo --

The main image consists of the letter V, which is the main letter of the name, symbolized in hare ears with the meaning that the hare because of how fast it can be, with the F that brings road routes where to travel , and a location symbol that will always be in use.

++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

-- Justification --

Its main purpose is to create a website where places to go out and have fun with the whole family will be recommended, all with the aim of reactivating the economic stability of small and large businesses, in addition to losing the social fear that was created in the pandemic.

Mission/Vision/Objectives/Impact

-- Mission --

VFAST seeks to create an online site where you can choose a place to visit safely, showing different functions within the site to make the visit pleasant, thus creating new and better experiences.

-- Vision --

To be the best online site helping to promote socioeconomic interest by promoting places; looking for the growth of small and medium establishments.

-- Objectives --

General: Maintain the sustainability of the places that have declined due to the pandemic, as well as reunite families, unite groups of friends, and motivate them to go out to places that they might find interesting.

Specific: Reach the devices of people of different ages, in order to lose the social panic that was generated in the pandemic, and the reactivation of recreation establishments.

-- Impact --

The loss of income in independent businesses and psychological problems are some of the problems caused by the isolation due to the Covid-19 pandemic. Based on these problems, a solution was sought, since it would help to give more recognition and popularity to those places that are hardly heard of, but that offer excellent services.
